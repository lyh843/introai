## 题目一

### (1)

由题目可知：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\hat{y_1} &= Sigmoid(\beta_1 - \theta_1) \\
&= \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(\beta_1 - \theta_1)}}
\end{aligned}
$$
因此可得：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial \hat{y_1}}{\partial \beta_1} &= -1 \times \frac{1}{(1 + e^{-(\beta_1 - \theta_1)})^2} e^{-(\beta_1 - \theta_1)} \times -1\\
&= \frac{e^{-(\beta_1 - \theta_1)}}{(1 + e^{-(\beta_1 - \theta_1)})^2}\\
&= (1 - \hat{y_1})\hat{y_1}
\end{aligned}
$$
对于$\ell(y, \hat{y_1}) = -[y \log(\hat{y_1}) + (1 - y)\log(1 - \hat{y_1})]$，有：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial \ell(y, \hat{y_1})}{\partial \hat{y_1}} &= -\frac{y}{\hat{y_1}} + \frac{(1 - y)}{1 - \hat{y_1}}\\
&= \frac{(\hat{y_1} - 1)y  + (1 - y)\hat{y_1}}{\hat{y_1}(1 - \hat{y_1})}\\
&= \frac{\hat{y_1} - y}{\hat{y_1}(1 - \hat{y_1})}
\end{aligned}
$$
因此，
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial \ell(y, \hat{y_1})}{\partial \beta_1} &= \frac{\partial \ell(y, \hat{y_1})}{\partial \hat{y_1}} \frac{\partial \hat{y_1}}{\partial \beta_1}\\
&= \frac{\hat{y_1} - y}{\hat{y_1}(1 - \hat{y_1})} (1 - \hat{y_1})\hat{y_1}\\
&= \hat{y_1} - y
\end{aligned}
$$

### (2) 

对于$\ell(y, \hat{y}) = -\sum_{j = 1}^l y_j \log\hat{y_j}$，有：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial \ell (y, \hat{y})}{\partial \hat{y}} &= (-\frac{y_1}{\hat{y_1}}, -\frac{y_2}{\hat{y_2}}, \cdots, -\frac{y_l}{\hat{y_l}})^T
\end{aligned}
$$
且有题目可知，
$$
\begin{aligned}
\hat{y} &= (\hat{y_1}, \hat{y_2}, \cdots, \hat{y_l})^T\\
&= (\frac{e^{\beta_1}}{\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k}}, \frac{e^{\beta_2}}{\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k}}, \cdots, \frac{e^{\beta_l}}{\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k}})\\
\end{aligned}
$$
因此对于单独的$\hat{y_j}$可得：

- 若$j = i$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial \hat{y_j}}{\partial \beta_i} &= \frac{e^{\beta_j}}{\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k}} - \frac{e^{\beta_j}}{(\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k})^2}e^{\beta_i}\\
&= \frac{e^{\beta_j}}{\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k}}\left(1 - \frac{e^{\beta_j}}{\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k}}\right)\\
&= \hat{y_j}(1 - \hat{y_j})
\end{aligned}
$$

- 若$j \neq i$
  $$
  \begin{aligned}
  \frac{\partial \hat{y_j}}{\partial \beta_i} &= - \frac{e^{\beta_j}}{(\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k})^2}e^{\beta_i}\\
  &= - \frac{e^{\beta_j}}{\sum_{k = 1}^l e^{\beta_k}} \frac{e^{\beta_i}}{\sum_{k=1}^l e^{\beta_k}}\\
  &= -\hat{y_j}\hat{y_i}
  \end{aligned}
  $$

- 

因此
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial \ell}{\partial \beta_k} &= \sum_{j = 1}^l \frac{\partial \ell}{\partial \hat{y_j}} \frac{\partial \hat{y_j}}{\partial \beta_k}\\
&= -\sum_{j = 1}^l \frac{y_j}{\hat{y_j}} \frac{\partial \hat{y_j}}{\partial \beta_k}\\
&= - \sum_{j = 1}^l
\begin{cases}
y_j(1 - \hat{y_j}), & j = k\\
- y_j\hat{y_k}, & j \neq k 
\end{cases}
\end{aligned}
$$
由于对于第$i$类样本有，$y_i = 1, y_j = 0, i \neq j$。

- 当$k = i$时，

$$
\frac{\partial \ell}{\partial \beta_k} = - y_i(1 - \hat{y_i}) = \hat{y_i} - 1 = \hat{y_i} - y_i
$$

- 当$k \neq i$时，
  $$
  \frac{\partial \ell}{\partial \beta_k} = -y_i\hat{y_k} = -\hat{y_k} = \hat{y_k} - y_k
  $$

因此
$$
\frac{\partial \ell}{\partial \beta} = (\hat{y_1} - y_1, \hat{y_2} - y_2, \cdots, \hat{y_n} - y_n)^T
$$

### (3)

在二分类问题中，通过问题(1)、(2)的计算可以发现二者的损失函数关于$\beta$的计算结果是一致的。

不同在于$sigmoid$激活函数在二分类问题中只会有一个输出，而$softmax$激活函数在二分类问题中会有两个输出。

但是考虑$softmax$激活函数在二分类下的公式：
$$
\begin{aligned}
y_1 &= \frac{e^{\beta_1}}{e^{\beta_1} + e^{\beta_2}}\\
&= \frac{1}{1 + e^{\beta_2 - \beta_1}}\\
&= \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(\beta_1 - \beta_2)}}
\end{aligned}
$$
令$\beta = \beta_1 - \beta_2$，则$softmax$激活函数也能转化为
$$
y_1 = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-\beta}}
$$
与$sigmoid$激活函数的形式一致，因此在二分类问题中，$softmax$本质上就是退化为了一个$sigmoid$函数

### (4)

对于$KL$散度公式，可转换为
$$
\begin{aligned}
D_{KL}(P \| Q) &= \sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}} P(x)\log\left(\frac{P(x)}{Q(x)}\right)\\
&= \sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}} P(x)\log(P(x)) - \sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}} P(X)\log(Q(x))\\
&=  -\sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}} P(x)\log\frac{1}{P(x)} + \sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}} P(X)\log\frac{1}{Q(x)}
\end{aligned}
$$
因为$\ell(y, \hat{y}) = \sum_{j = 1}^l y_j\log \frac{1}{\hat{y_j}}$， $\ell(y, y) = \sum_{j = 1}^l y_j \log \frac{1}{y_j}$

因此令$y_j = P(x), \hat{y_j} = Q(x)$，有
$$
D_{KL}(P \| Q) = \ell(y, \hat{y}) - \ell(y, y)
$$

## 题目二

### (1)

对于第$i$层而言$i = 0, 1$：

输入$X_i$，前向传递：$X_{i + 1} = \sigma(W_i X_i + b_i)$.

因此
$$
X_3 = W_2\sigma(W_1\sigma(W_0X_0 + b_0) + b_1) + b_2
$$
最后计算损失：
$$
\ell = \frac{1}{2ml} \|X_3 - Y\|_F^2
$$

### (2)

对于$\ell$，可进行进一步转换：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\ell &= \frac{1}{2ml} \|X_3 - Y \|_F^2 \\
&= \frac{1}{2ml} \sum_{ij}(X_{3,ij} - Y_{ij})^2
\end{aligned}
$$
反向传播：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial \ell}{\partial X_{3, ij}} &= \frac{1}{2ml}2(X_{3, ij} - Y_{ij})\\
&= \frac{1}{ml}(X_{3, ij} - Y_{ij})
\end{aligned}
$$
由题目一可知，若$Y = \sigma(x)$，则$Y' = (1 - Y)Y$.

因此进一步可得：
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial X_{3, ij}}{\partial W_2} &= X_2\\
\frac{\partial X_{3, ij}}{\partial b_2} &= 1\\
\frac{\partial X_{3, ij}}{\partial X_{2, ij}} &= W_2\\
\frac{\partial X_{2, ij}}{\partial W_1} &= (1 - X_2)X_2X_1\\
\frac{\partial X_{2, ij}}{\partial b_1} &= (1 - X_2)X_2\\
\frac{\partial X_{2, ij}}{\partial X_{1, ij}} &= (1 - X_2)X_2 W_1\\
\frac{\partial X_{1, ij}}{\partial W_0} &= (1 - X_1)X_1X_0\\
\frac{\partial x_{1, ij}}{\partial b_0} &= (1 - X_1)X_1\\
\end{aligned}
$$
